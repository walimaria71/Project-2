Recipe of the day:Spegheti Carbonara


Spegheti carbonara is a classic italian pasta dish made with egg, cheese,pancetta and pepper. Its simple,delicious and perfect for any ocassion.

Ingredients


  • 200g speghetti
    100g pancetta
    2 large eggs
    50g Pecorino cheese
    Black pepper
    Salt

    • Steps to Prepare


      1.Boiled the spegetti insalted water until all dente
      2.Fry the pancetta until cricpy
      3.Beat the egg and mix with grated cheeses
      4.Drain the speghrtti and mix with pancetta
      5.Remove from heat ans stir in the egg and cheese mixture
      6.Season with black pepper and serve immediately
